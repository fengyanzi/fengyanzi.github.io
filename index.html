Xinyu Yan


I am a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence and Weaponry Engineering at Beijing Institute of Technology. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Intelligent Unmanned Systems Technology from Beijing Institute of Technology, during which I received multiple Academic Scholarships and the Aviation Industry Scholarship.

My primary research focuses on image processing, particularly visual tasks such as cloud removal, denoising, and super-resolution, as well as weak target detection and signal diagnosis. Additionally, I have a keen interest in conditional diffusion models, large language models, full-stack web development, and hardware/embedded systems, striving to integrate cross-disciplinary technologies to advance AI applications in real-world scenarios.
